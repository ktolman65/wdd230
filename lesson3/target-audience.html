Audience

Primary Target Profile

  • Gender: Male and Female
  • Age: 25 – 35
  • Education: Associates
  • Relationship: Single
  • Children in household: No
  • Income: $50,000 +
  • Media Consumption: Facebook, Online Media
  • Internet Search Device Primary: Mobile

Persona: Lisa Ollin

Lisa Ollin is a single, twenty-eight (28) year old remote worker for a Silicon Valley-based tech company. She works long hours as a college recruiter searching for graduates of computer science with entry-level programming experience. Her company supplied her with the latest technology, including the latest smart phone, her primary device for interacting with recruits, peers and the web.

Lisa wants a website that provides her with event information to plan time with her friends and community.

Secondary Target Profile

  • Gender: Male and Female
  • Age: 35 – 44
  • Education: Associates
  • Relationship: Married
  • Children in household: Yes
  • Income: $75,000 +
  • Media Consumption: Magazines, Facebook
  • Internet Search Device Primary: Laptop

Persona: Steve Halliday

Steve Halliday is a married, forty-two (42) year old mechanic. He and his wife of twenty (17) years have two children, ages seven (7) and nine (9).

Steve needs a website that can quickly advise him of local family-friendly events.
