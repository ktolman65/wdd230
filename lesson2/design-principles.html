Design Principles

Ken Tolman

Contrast

AllTrails

alltrails.com

AllTrails creates it's easy to read website by using contrast. Excellent contract is created by placing dark and colorful images and dark text over a plain white background.

White Space

America First

americafirst.com

America First uses white space to focus the users attention to the specific design elements.

Alignment

Disney+

disneyplus.com

Disney+ creates visual interest through the use of alignment. The main images and text are aligned along the left side of the page. The sub categories are also aligned left to right to provide a grouping effect.
