MICC

Site Purpose

MICC - The (Montpelier Idaho Chamber of Commerce) website promotes the business interests of the members of the organization and promote the area, attracting additional business, tourism, and people.

Content Objectives

  1. The design is consistent throughout the website providing ease of navigation and supporting positive user experiences.
  2. The site supports responsive design techniques allowing delivery of the site and its content on all classes of devices—large-wide screens (desktop/laptop), medium-screens (tablet), and small-screens (smartphones).
  3. The header contains a logo, site title, navigation, and social media links.
  4. The footer requirements include contact information and a map link. The content requirements will be specific.
  5. The navigation menu contains links to the following pages: Home, Discover, Directory, Join, and Contact.
  • Home page: The home page will contain some attention grabbing information along with community information, weather, calls to action, and business spotlights.
  • Discover page: The discover page provides historical information about the town and current demographics. Use imagery and include any relevant city events and information.
  • Directory page: This page provides a list of local business organizations that are located in the area.
  • Join page: This page contains information about the benefit of joining the chamber and a membership application form. The form will have specific content requirements to be assigned during the course. There are four membership level options of non-profit, bronze, silver, and gold. The different levels have different perks including directory listings, publications, trainings, social media work, luncheons, sponsorships, advertising, etc.
  • Contact page: The contact page contains essential and extended contact information for the chamber and its officers and board of directors. This page will have a simple contact us form asking for name, phone, email, and a comment/message area. The page will have some other developmental specifications to provide opportunities for learning.
